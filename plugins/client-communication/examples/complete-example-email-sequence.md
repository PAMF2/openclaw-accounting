## What Makes This Sequence Effective

1. **Specificity**: Every email lists exactly what is needed — no vague "send your documents"
2. **Escalating consequences**: Each email adds more concrete information about what happens if nothing changes
3. **Offers of help**: Emails 1-2 offer calls and portal walkthroughs — the problem might be confusion, not negligence
4. **Professional throughout**: Even the final notice is firm without being hostile — preserves the client relationship
5. **Actionable deadlines**: Every email has a specific date and a specific action
6. **Penalty math**: Email 3 introduces the $220/shareholder/month penalty — real numbers motivate action
7. **Decision-making in Email 4**: Rather than pleading, it shifts to "here is what we will do" — takes the decision off the client's plate

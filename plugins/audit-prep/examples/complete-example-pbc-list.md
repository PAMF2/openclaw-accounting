# Complete Example: Audit PBC List

## Input

**Client Profile:**
- Company: Precision Components Manufacturing Inc.
- Industry: Custom metal parts manufacturing (aerospace and automotive)
- Revenue: $25,000,000 (calendar year 2025)
- Entity type: C-Corporation
- Fiscal year-end: December 31, 2025
- Audit type: First-year audit (new audit engagement)
- Prior accountant: Compiled financial statements only (no prior audit)
- Locations: 1 manufacturing facility (owned), 1 leased warehouse
- Employees: 145 FTE
- Key systems: NetSuite ERP, ADP Payroll, Bank of America (operating), Wells Fargo (payroll)
- Audit firm materiality (estimated): $250,000 overall / $125,000 performance materiality
- Special considerations: Government contracts (DCAA compliance), inventory-heavy, significant fixed assets
